Color Perception

Coloring Method of Black-and-White High-Speed Projection Images

These days, a projector which projects 8-bit gray images at 1,000 frames per second was developed and came to be available for consumers. However, it cannot project full-color images. Therefore, we propose a system which enables us to perceive colored images using a black-and-white projector without losing its speed of projection by applying Land’s two-color method, which claims that images made from only two light sources (white and red) can be seen colorfully. We integrated this method with our optical system in which two images superimposed at the same time from a projector combine on the screen after being reflected by mirrors.
