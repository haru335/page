Level-Set Method

Estimation of Contribution of Defects on Fuel Cells by Multilayer Level Set Functions

It is known that some type of fuel cells tend to be degrade over time and would not pass enough electric current.

We developed a method to detect such areas where current density is low by introducing multilayer level set functions. Multiple level set functions act at the same time to divide 2D areas in stages. As a result, we confirmed that some defects could be detected more accurately than any previous method.
