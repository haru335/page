Color Perception

Beautiful images with full color always move our minds. Visualization of colors even under limited lighting environments would lead to extension of our whole experiences.

User-Oriented Displays

Seeing what we want to see is important factor in the more and more information-driven society. What if you could get only necessary images without being interrupted by others?

Effects of Delays

Immersive experiences or sensitive operations should not be affected by delays induced by visual feedback systems. Human performances can be investigated using high-speed imagings.
